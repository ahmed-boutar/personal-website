About Tap The Color

TapTheColor is a game built using Swift and xcode, designed to provide an enjoyable and challenging experience for players. The game requires users to tap the correct color based on a given target color, with the colors randomized and the target color changing every six seconds. The app has been designed to work on devices with different screen sizes, ensuring that users can enjoy the game regardless of the device they are using. Additionally, the app has been localized to French.


TapTheColor is designed to provide a fun and challenging experience for players, with different levels of difficulty to keep them engaged. Each level has a different difficulty, with the speed of rotations between colors increasing as players progress, making it harder to score points.
